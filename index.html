Server Rules & Guidelines

Important Notice

Violation of these rules may result in temporary suspension or permanent ban from our server.

General Rules

Respect all players and staff members. Harassment or discrimination will not be tolerated.

No cheating, hacking, or use of any third-party software that provides an unfair advantage.

No spamming in chat (text or voice), including advertising other servers.

Roleplay appropriately - stay in character and follow the server's roleplay guidelines.

Roleplay Specific Rules

No metagaming (using out-of-character knowledge in character).

No powergaming (forcing actions on other players without giving them a chance to respond).

No random deathmatching (RDM) - all hostile actions must have proper roleplay justification.

Follow all faction rules and guidelines if you're part of an official faction.
